What runways to choose depending on the winds

If the winds are less than 5 knots you should depart "South" using the 18s and 17s

If the winds are 5 knots or greater and the wind direciton is 90-270° you should depart 17s and 18s

If the winds are 5 knots or greather and the wind direction is 271-89° you should depart 35s and 36s
