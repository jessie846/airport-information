Runway Information at KMCO







Runway 17L/35R

The Runway is 12005 x 200 feet / 3659 x 61 meters long and has an ILS

This Runway is primarily used for arrivals however sometimes can be used for departures if runway 35L/17R is closed.



Runway 17R/35L

The Runway is 10000 ft x 150 ft / 3048 m x 45.7 m long and has ILS

THis Runway is primarily used for departures however sometimes if busy on arrivals Orlando Approach may put some aircraft on this runway for arrival.

Runway 18L/36R

The Runway is 12005 x 200 ft / 3659 x 61 m long and has an ILS

This Runway is only used for departures

Runway 18R/36L

The Runway is 12004 ft x 200 ft 3659 m x 61 m long and has an ILS on Runway 18R, however only has up to an RNAV Approach for Runway 36l.

The Runway is used for primarily arrivals however, for aircraft departing from the west Side of the airfiled ( for example west ramp and fedex ramp etc. ) they can most of the time can expect RWY 36L for departure.
