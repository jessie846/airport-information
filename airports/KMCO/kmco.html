Runway/Taxi Configuration

Taxiway rules

Orlando taxis departure aircraft on the outer taxiways, C and G. They taxi arrival aircraft via B and and H

Taxiway E is only for aircraf taxiing east and Taxiway F is for aircraft taxxing to the West

Runway Rules

Runway 36L, 35R, 18R, 17L are used for arrivals

Runway 36R, 35L, 17R, 18L are used for departures
