KMCO METAR and TAF


Runway selection based on winds

If the winds are less than 5 knots you should use the 24s.

If the winds are 5 knots or greater and the wind direciton is 150-330° you should depart/arrive the 24s

If the winds are 5 knots or greater and the wind direction is 331-149° you should depart/arrive the 6s
