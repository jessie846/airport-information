Runway selection based on winds

If the winds are less than 5 knots you should use the 27's.

If the winds are 5 knots or greater and the wind direciton is 180-360° you should depart/arrive the 27s

If the winds are 5 knots or greater and the wind direction is 001-179° you should depart/arrive the 9s
